Bakerbots Baking

The macaron. A simple and classic french pastry. Naturally gluten free, these lovelies are made with almond flour. They come in a variety of flavours and colours to match. Joli Macarons sources all of its ingredients from award winning purveyors from Canada and Europe. We don't use synthetic flavourings. In fact we use fresh organic fruits for our fillings when possible. Yes, they're pretty to look at. But we focus on flavour and delivering an "Oh My Gosh" moment in every bite. It's what's inside that counts.

“Macarons only weigh a few grams, but that’s enough to leave your senses quivering with pleasure. Their thin, crisp shell, slightly rounded shape, tempting colours and tender interiors draw devotees to devour them with their eyes, and caress their smooth surface. Their flavours solicit the nose and, when one bites into that crisp shell, the ears tingle with pleasure the palate is finally rewarded.”
